Structure-based Kinases Resources: KPDB, KLPDB, AKPDB, and CIKPDB

The Structure-based Kinase Resources is a comprehensive collection of all published kinase domain structures from the UniProt and the Protein Data Bank (PDB). It consists of four curated components: the Kinase Domain PDB database (KPDB), Kinase Ligand-binding PDB database (KLPDB), Apo Kinase PDB database (AKPDB), and Covalent Inhibitor-binding Kinase PDB database (CIKPDB).
